DNT Certificate in Fluoride Varnish Application
CPD Activity Details
CPD Subject Fluoride Varnish Application
Learning Content Fluoride science and caries prevention; patient assessment and communication; clinical application and safety; professional responsibilities and law; managing patient reluctance to fluoride. Assessment via MCQs, written tasks, clinical logbook (10 supervised applications), case studies, and a final reflective account.
Aims To develop the knowledge, understanding and clinical skills required to safely apply fluoride varnish as a dental nurse extended duty, in accordance with GDC Scope of Practice guidelines and current evidence-based practice.
Objectives On completion, learners are able to: (1) explain the science of fluoride and mechanisms of caries prevention; (2) conduct appropriate patient assessment and obtain valid consent; (3) safely apply fluoride varnish following current clinical evidence; (4) demonstrate compliance with GDC professional and legal requirements; (5) manage patient concerns and reluctance regarding fluoride treatment.
Anticipated GDC Development Outcomes C - Patient safety and quality of care: safe clinical application, risk assessment, consent and record-keeping.
D - Maintenance and development of knowledge and skills: evidence-based theory, professional responsibilities, reflective practice.
