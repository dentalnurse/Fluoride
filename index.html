Assessment

How you are assessed

No exams. Assessment is through MCQs, written tasks, your clinical logbook, and a final reflective account. All submitted and reviewed online.

📝 MCQ - 15 questions per module

A 15-question multiple choice quiz at the end of each module. You must pass before moving to the next module. You can retake if needed.

✍️ Written tasks (x3)

Three written tasks on consent, GDC registrant responsibilities, and fluoride science. Download the task sheet, complete it, and upload your answers.

🦷 Clinical logbook (10 entries)

Log each supervised fluoride varnish application, including patient group, caries risk, product used, prescription confirmation, medical considerations and reflection. Upload completed logbook pages.

📋 Case studies (x2)

Two extended case studies covering full patient assessment, consent, application technique, challenges and reflection. Upload as part of your practical portfolio.

🪞 End-of-course reflective account (300–500 words)

A final reflective account drawing on your learning and clinical experience across the course. Written directly in the platform or uploaded as a document.

GDC Scope of Practice

Aligned with GDC Scope of Practice (November 2025)

The GDC confirms that dental nurses may apply fluoride varnish under the prescription of a dentist, or as part of a structured dental health programme, provided they are appropriately trained, competent and indemnified. This qualification supports all three requirements. It remains the responsibility of each individual dental nurse to ensure they are trained, competent and appropriately indemnified before undertaking this procedure.

The NHS Amendment Regulations 2026 (in force 1 April 2026) formally recognise standalone fluoride varnish delivery by a dental nurse as an NHS course of treatment. This qualification positions you to take on that role.
