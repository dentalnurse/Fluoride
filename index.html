About the course

Extend your scope.
Enhance your practice.

The DNT Certificate in Fluoride Varnish Application provides the theoretical foundation and demonstrated clinical competence required for dental nurses to apply fluoride varnish under the prescription of a dentist, or as part of a structured oral health programme.

Delivered by Em Bremner — CAVA, AET, NEBDN Dental Nursing and OHE — this qualification is fully aligned with GDC Scope of Practice guidance (November 2025) and the NHS dental contract reforms of 2026.

Course overview
📋

Blended learning

Online video/lecture theory content across three modules, combined with a workplace-based portfolio of evidence.

🦷

Clinical competence

10 supervised applications across five patient groups, each witnessed and signed off by a GDC-registered supervisor.

📝

Portfolio assessment

Three written tasks, an extended case study of 1,000–1,500 words, and a reflective log for each application.

🎓

DNT certificate

A named certificate from DNT on successful completion, supporting your personal scope of practice as a GDC registrant.
