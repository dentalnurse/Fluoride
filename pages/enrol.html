Before you enquire

Entry requirements

You must hold current GDC registration, have at least 6 months post-registration experience, be in active clinical practice, and have access to patients across all five required groups.

Supervisor

You need a named GDC-registered supervisor (dentist, hygienist or therapist) who is qualified in fluoride varnish application and willing to directly observe all 10 applications.

Indemnity

Check with your indemnity provider before enrolling to confirm your policy covers extended duties including fluoride varnish application.

Contact

You can also reach Em directly via dentalnurse.training or through the DNT social media channels.
