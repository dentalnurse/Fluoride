Course Documents

All documents are provided to enrolled learners. Contact DNT to enquire and access your pack.

All documents are provided in Word (.docx) and PDF format. Enrolled learners receive the full document pack on enrolment.

01

Course Information Sheet

Full course overview including structure, entry requirements, supervisor requirements, and learner agreement sign-off. The first document learners and their supervisor should read.

Learner
02

Course Handbook

Full theory content across all three modules, including legislation, cross infection control, fluoride varnish science, patient management, application technique, communication and managing patients who decline fluoride. Also available as an interactive presentation on this website.

Learner
03

Portfolio of Evidence

The primary assessment document. Contains: 10 application logs with competency checklists and supervisor sign-off, three written task pages with prompts and guidance, the extended case study template with structured sections, and the portfolio summary declaration.

Learner
04

Assessor Marking Guide

Confidential DNT internal document. Contains marking criteria for the application log review, all three written tasks and the case study, plus the overall PASS/Not Yet Pass decision sheet.

Assessor only
05

Completion Certificate

Awarded on successful completion of the full portfolio assessment. Includes Em Bremner's qualifications (CAVA, AET, NEBDN, OHE) and a GDC compliance statement. Issued as a named, dated document with a unique certificate number.

On completion
06

Witness Declaration

Must be read and signed by the clinical supervisor before any applications take place. Sets out supervisor eligibility, responsibilities during and after each application, the consequences of falsely signing off work not observed, indemnity requirements, and patient safety obligations.

Supervisor must sign
How to access documents
