Employer and Employee Handbook

These handbooks provide guidance on the responsibilities of employers and employees in relation to the DNT Fluoride Varnish Application Certificate programme.

These handbooks provide guidance on the responsibilities of employers and employees in relation to the DNT Fluoride Varnish Application Certificate programme. They are designed to support practices in implementing fluoride varnish application as an extended duty for dental nurses.

🏢

Employer Handbook

Guidance for dental practices and employers on supporting dental nurses undertaking the DNT Fluoride Varnish Application Certificate, including supervision requirements and governance.

👩‍⚕️

Employee Handbook

Guidance for dental nurses on the requirements and responsibilities for undertaking and completing the DNT Fluoride Varnish Application Certificate, including CPD and GDC compliance.
