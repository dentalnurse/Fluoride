Extenuating Circumstances

If personal circumstances have prevented you from completing your course, you can apply for a free extension. We will review your application and respond within 2 working days.

Before you apply: Extenuating circumstances are serious, unexpected events outside your control — such as significant illness, bereavement, or a family emergency. Work pressure or general busyness is not usually accepted. We will review each application individually and in confidence.
